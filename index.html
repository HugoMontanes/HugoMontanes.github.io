Hugo Montañés

Technical Artist & VFX Specialist

I'm a passionate Technical Artist specializing in shader development, visual effects, and real-time rendering. Graduated with a degree in Video Game Design and Development at UDIT (Universidad de Diseño, Innovación y Tecnología).

My expertise lies in bridging the gap between art and technology. I create stunning visual experiences through advanced shader programming in Adobe Substance Designer and Unreal Engine, develop immersive VFX systems using Niagara, and optimize rendering pipelines for real-time applications. I'm proficient in C++, C#, and Blueprints, allowing me to implement complex technical art solutions.

Throughout my academic journey and project work, I've embraced SCRUM methodology for agile project management and utilize Git/GitHub for version control and team collaboration, ensuring efficient workflows and seamless team coordination.
